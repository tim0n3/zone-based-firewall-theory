3. ZBF Matrix Logic (Forward Chain)

The core of the Zone-Based Firewall is the jump matrix. Instead of evaluating 50+ rules sequentially, the router checks the connection state, jumps to a highly specific mini-chain based on the In/Out interfaces, and accepts or drops the packet rapidly.
